About Me

Hi. I'm Chelsi and I'm a visual, audio and print journalist who loves data mining and dabbles in coding. My work has been published in the New York Times, Washington Post and Chicago's WBEZ. I was born in Montana, but today I live in the San Francisco Bay Area and study multimedia, visual journalism and data visualization at UC Berkeley's Graduate School of Journalism. When I'm not working, I'm outside.
